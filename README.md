## Brief Description

Have you ever been in a situation where someone says, "I'll pay you back later," but later never really comes? Whether it's splitting a restaurant bill, sharing a taxi, or traveling with friends, keeping track of who owes whom often becomes confusing and frustrating.

OweBuddy is a web application designed to help users track shared expenses within groups such as friends, roommates, or travel companions. The system automatically calculates balances, determines who owes whom, and simplifies debt settlement through intelligent expense tracking and notifications.
